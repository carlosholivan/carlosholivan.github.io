Music Composition Evaluation Survey

Thank you for participating in this survey!

This online survey aims to evaluate the music generated by Deep Learning models. It will take you around 10 minutes to complete the survey.

You will listen to 8 samples in which have been either generated from A.I. models or are taken from different databases of human-composed sequences.
Please, don't pay attention to the sound quality of the samples you will listeng to, but to the composition itself.

To start with the test, click the user level you identfy with and you will be directed to the survey. You will have to accept the consent statement before listening to the samples and completing the questions.

What user level are you?

Please, be careful with the option you choose, surveys vary between user levels.

Begginer Level: I haven't studied music / I'm an amateur musician with no experience.

Intermediate Level: I have studied music / I'm a music producer / I know some music theory.

Advanced Level: I'm a professional composer / I'm music teacher at a conservatory or music school / I'm studying a Bachelor or Masters degree in music.
