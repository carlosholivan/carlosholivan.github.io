C. Hernandez-Olivan

PhD in Music Technology & Deep Learning

Email | GitHub | Google Scholar | LinkedIn

Hi, I am C. Hernandez-Olivan from Zaragoza, Spain, a historic city known for its Roman heritage. I am passionate about music and cinema.

Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at NTT. My research interests include:

  • Source separation/Target sound extraction (general sounds, audio tagging, sound event detection)
  • Spatial Audio
  • Music restoration (audio enhancement, bandwidth extension, declipping)
  • Music generation (symbolic and audio with generative models)
  • Music structure analysis (boundary detection and graph representations)
